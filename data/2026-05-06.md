### [1] [Beyond Similarity Search: A Unified Data Layer for Production RAG Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03275)
*Venkata Krishna Prasanth Budigi,Siri Chandana Sirigiri*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文指出传统RAG系统因数据层分离导致的数据陈旧、租户数据泄露和查询组合爆炸三大问题，并提出基于PostgreSQL（pgvector+HNSW索引）的统一数据层方案，在5万文档基准上实现92%延迟降低、零同步不一致和完全消除跨租户数据泄露。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 生产环境中RAG系统在原型阶段表现良好，但实际部署时可靠性显著下降，根本原因在于传统分离式数据层架构存在数据陈旧、租户数据泄露和查询组合爆炸三大问题。

Method: 提出基于PostgreSQL的统一数据层架构，利用pgvector原生向量搜索和HNSW索引替代传统分离式数据层（如独立的向量数据库+关系数据库），减少同步环节。

Result: 在5万文档的受控基准测试中，日期过滤查询延迟降低92%，租户作用域查询延迟降低74%，零同步不一致，完全消除跨租户数据泄露，同步代码减少93%。

Conclusion: 统一数据层方案能有效解决RAG系统生产环境中的可靠性问题，并建议采用混合分层架构来进一步优化。

Abstract: Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems have become the standard architecture for grounding large language models in organizational knowledge. Yet production deployments consistently expose a gap between clean prototype performance and real-world reliability. This paper identifies three root causes of that gap: data staleness, tenant data leakage, and query composition explosion. All three trace back to the conventional split-system data layer. We propose and evaluate a unified data layer built on PostgreSQL with native vector search (pgvector) and HNSW indexing. Controlled benchmarks on 50,000 documents show 92% latency reduction for date-filtered queries, 74% for tenant-scoped queries, zero synchronization inconsistency, and complete elimination of cross-tenant data leakage with 93% less synchronization code. We additionally discuss a recommended hybrid tier architecture

### [2] [RAG over Thinking Traces Can Improve Reasoning Tasks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03344)
*Negar Arabzadeh,Wenjie Ma,Sewon Min,Matei Zaharia*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文挑战了"RAG对推理任务帮助有限"的普遍假设，提出通过检索思考轨迹（thinking traces）而非文档来增强推理能力。作者引入T3方法将思考轨迹转化为结构化、易检索的表示，在AIME、LiveCodeBench等推理基准上显著提升性能，相对提升最高达+56.3%，且几乎不增加推理成本。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有RAG方法主要检索文档，被认为对数学、代码等推理密集型任务帮助有限。作者质疑这一假设，认为问题不在RAG本身，而在于检索语料的选择——文档不适合推理任务，而思考轨迹（问题求解过程中的中间推理步骤）可能更有效。

Method: 1) 提出使用思考轨迹（thinking traces）作为检索语料；2) 引入T3方法，将思考轨迹离线转化为结构化、紧凑、易于检索的表示；3) 采用简单的"检索-生成"流水线：先检索相关思考轨迹，再将其作为上下文输入模型进行推理。

Result: 在AIME 2025-2026、LiveCodeBench、GPQA-Diamond等推理基准上，RAG+思考轨迹一致优于非RAG基线和基于网络文档的检索。以AIME为例，使用Gemini-2-thinking生成的轨迹，对Gemini-2.5-Flash、GPT-OSS-120B、GPT-5分别实现+56.3%、+8.6%、+7.6%的相对提升。T3方法几乎不增加推理成本，甚至可降低最多15%的推理成本。

Conclusion: 思考轨迹是推理任务的有效检索语料，将其转化为结构化、紧凑或诊断性表示可以解锁更强的性能增益。该发现挑战了RAG对推理任务无用的普遍认知，为推理增强提供了新路径。

Abstract: Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) has proven effective for knowledge-intensive tasks, but is widely believed to offer limited benefit for reasoning-intensive problems such as math and code generation. We challenge this assumption by showing that the limitation lies not in RAG itself, but in the choice of corpus. Instead of retrieving documents, we propose retrieving thinking traces, i.e., intermediate thinking trajectories generated during problem solving attempts. We show that thinking traces are already a strong retrieval source, and further introduce T3, an offline method that transforms them into structured, retrieval-friendly representations, to improve usability. Using these traces as a corpus, a simple retrieve-then-generate pipeline consistently improves reasoning performance across strong models and benchmarks such as AIME 2025--2026, LiveCodeBench, and GPQA-Diamond, outperforming both non-RAG baselines and retrieval over standard web corpora. For instance, on AIME, RAG with traces generated by Gemini-2-thinking achieves relative gains of +56.3%, +8.6%, and +7.6% for Gemini-2.5-Flash, GPT-OSS-120B, and GPT-5, respectively, even though these are more recent models. Interestingly, RAG on T3 also incurs little or no extra inference cost, and can even reduce inference cost by up to $15%$. Overall, our results suggest that thinking traces are an effective retrieval corpus for reasoning tasks, and transforming them into structured, compact, or diagnostic representations unlocks even stronger gains. Code available at https://github.com/Narabzad/t3.

### [3] [Revisiting General Map Search via Generative Point-of-Interest Retrieval](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03397)
*Dong Chen,Shuai Zheng,Haoyang Shao,Hongsheng Wu,Muhao Xu,Yeyu Yan,Ruifang Li,Zhenfeng Zhu*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: GenPOI是一种针对地图通用搜索的生成式POI检索框架，通过将异构上下文和POI统一为结构化序列，利用大语言模型的上下文建模能力，结合地理语义标记化和邻近感知约束生成策略，在千万级POI数据集上取得优异性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统POI检索方法过度依赖表面语义匹配，难以应对用户查询不明确、高度依赖上下文和个性化需求的问题，现有检索范式无法有效整合异构上下文进行复杂搜索意图推理。

Method: 1) 将异构搜索上下文和POI统一为结构化序列；2) 提出Geo-Semantic POI Tokenization，将每个POI编码为包含语义和地理上下文的紧凑标记序列；3) 采用邻近感知约束生成策略，限制LLM解码空间，确保结果的有效性和地理空间相关性。

Result: 在腾讯地图千万级POI的大规模工业数据集上，GenPOI相较于传统方法展现出优越性能。

Conclusion: 生成式范式通过深度上下文依赖建模和搜索意图推理，能有效解决更具挑战性的查询问题，为地图通用搜索提供了新思路。

Abstract: Point-of-Interest (POI) retrieval aims to identify relevant candidates from massive-scale POI databases, serving as a cornerstone for diverse location-based services. However, in general map search scenarios, conventional POI retrieval methods are increasingly challenged by underspecified user queries due to their excessive reliance on surface-level semantic matching. Meanwhile, such queries are often highly context-dependent and personalized, yet existing retrieval paradigms struggle to effectively synergize heterogeneous contexts for complex search intent inference. To address these limitations, we revisit general map search from a generative perspective and propose GenPOI, an innovative Generative POI retrieval framework tailored for general search on maps. It seamlessly unifies heterogeneous search contexts and POIs into structured sequences, leveraging the powerful contextual modeling of Large Language Models (LLMs) for spatial-aware candidate generation. Consequently, this generative paradigm effectively solves more challenging queries through profound context dependency modeling and search intent reasoning. Specifically, accounting for the unique geospatial nature of map scenarios, GenPOI introduces a novel Geo-Semantic POI Tokenization to represent each POI as a compact token sequence encoding both semantic and geographic context, thus grounding the LLM's spatial understanding. Additionally, a proximity-aware constrained generation strategy is employed to restrict the decoding space of the LLM, ensuring the validity and geospatial relevance of the generated results. Extensive experiments on large-scale industrial datasets from Tencent Map, comprising POIs at the scale of over 10 million, demonstrate the superior performance of GenPOI.

### [4] [Aspect-Aware Content-Based Recommendations for Mathematical Research Papers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03861)
*Ankit Satpute,André Greiner-Petter,Noah Gießing,Olaf Teschke,Moritz Schubotz,Akiko Aizawa,Bela Gipp*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文提出AchGNN，一种面向数学文献的方面感知异构GNN推荐系统，通过联合建模文本语义、引用结构和作者谱系来解决数学论文概念性关联强但文本/引用重叠少的问题，在构建的GoldRiM和SilverRiM数据集上显著优于现有方法，并成功迁移至机器学习领域。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有基于内容的研究论文推荐在计算机科学和生物医学领域取得进展，但数学领域尚未被探索。数学论文之间的关联更多体现为共享证明技术、逻辑蕴含或自然推广等概念层面，而非显式的文本或引用相似度，导致现有CbRPR方法失效。

Method: 首先通过专家驱动研究揭示数学推荐本质上是方面驱动的（aspect-driven）；然后构建GoldRiM（小规模专家标注）和SilverRiM（大规模自动构建）两个方面感知数据集；提出AchGNN，一种方面条件化的异构GNN，联合建模文本语义、引用结构和作者谱系。

Result: 在GoldRiM和SilverRiM上，AchGNN在所有评估方面上均显著优于先前方法；消融实验验证了方面监督、作者谱系和图结构信号的各自贡献；在Papers with Code机器学习数据集上验证了跨领域泛化能力。

Conclusion: 方面感知的异构GNN方法能有效解决数学文献推荐中的概念关联问题，且具有良好的领域迁移性，已部署在MaRDI平台上服务数学研究者。

Abstract: Content-based research paper recommendation (CbRPR) has seen advances in computer science and biomedicine, but remains unexplored for mathematics, where paper relatedness is more conceptual than explicit textual or citation-based similarity. Mathematics papers may be connected through shared proof techniques, logical implications, or natural generalizations, yet exhibit minimal textual or citation overlap, rendering existing CbRPR ineffective. To address this gap, we first conduct an expert-driven study characterizing mathematical recommendations, revealing that relevance is inherently \textit{aspect}-driven. Grounded in this insight, we introduce GoldRiM (small, expert-annotated) and SilverRiM (large, automatically derived), the first datasets for \textit{aspect}-aware CbRPR in mathematics. Recognizing that LLM embeddings of mathematical content alone yield suboptimal representation, we propose AchGNN, an \textit{aspect}-conditioned heterogeneous GNN that jointly models textual semantics, citation structure, and author lineage. Across GoldRiM and SilverRiM, AchGNN consistently outperforms prior \textit{aspect}-based CbRPR methods, achieving substantial gains across all evaluated \textit{aspects}. We conduct ablation studies to analyze the contributions of individual \textit{aspect} supervision, authorship lineage, and graph-structural signals to AchGNN's performance. To assess domain generality, we further evaluate AchGNN on the \textit{Papers with Code} dataset of machine learning publications, demonstrating that our \textit{aspect}-aware approach effectively transfers beyond mathematics. We deploy our system on the MaRDI platform to help mathematicians with recommendations and release datasets and code publicly for reproducibility.

### [5] [Domain-Adaptive Dense Retrieval for Brazilian Legal Search](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04005)
*Jayr Pereira,Roberto Lotufo,Luiz Bonifacio*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文研究了巴西法律检索中密集检索器的专业化与鲁棒性之间的权衡，比较了基于Qwen3-Embedding-4B的三种训练设置：基础模型、纯法律数据训练模型、以及法律数据+SQuAD-pt混合训练模型。混合模型在保持法律任务性能的同时，整体平衡性更好，在NDCG@10、MRR@10和MAP@10三个指标上均有提升，尤其在问答式检索任务上优势明显。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 巴西法律检索具有异质性（涵盖判例法、立法和基于问题的搜索），训练密集检索器需要在更强的领域专业化和更广泛的检索类型鲁棒性之间做出权衡。作者旨在探索这一权衡，寻找既能保持法律专业能力又能在不同检索类型中表现稳健的训练策略。

Method: 基于Qwen3-Embedding-4B模型，设置三种训练方案：(1) 基础模型（无微调）；(2) 仅用法律数据微调；(3) 混合训练（法律数据 + SQuAD-pt监督数据集）。在JUÁ排行榜的五个法律数据集和Quati数据集（额外葡萄牙语检索基准）上评估，使用NDCG@10、MRR@10、MAP@10指标。

Result: 纯法律模型在最专业化的法律任务上表现最佳；混合模型在六个数据集上的平均NDCG@10从0.414提升至0.447，MRR@10从0.586提升至0.595，MAP@10从0.270提升至0.308。最大改进出现在Quati数据集上，混合模型明显优于纯法律模型。

Conclusion: 纯法律训练和专业训练各有优势：前者更适合专业化任务，后者在多种检索类型（尤其是基于问题的搜索）上更具鲁棒性。两种微调模型均已发布在Hugging Face上。

Abstract: Brazilian legal retrieval is heterogeneous, covering case law, legislation, and question-based search. This makes training dense retrievers a trade-off between stronger domain specialization and broader robustness across retrieval types of search. In this paper, we explore this trade-off using three training setups based on Qwen3-Embedding-4B: a base model with no fine-tuning, a version trained only on legal data, and a mixed setup that combines legal data with SQuAD-pt supervised dataset. We evaluate these models on five legal datasets from the JUÁ leaderboard, along with Quati dataset as an extra Portuguese retrieval benchmark to test out-of-domain generalization. The legal-only model performs best on the most specialized legal tasks. The mixed setup keeps strong performance on legal data while offering a better overall balance, improving average NDCG@10 from 0.414 to 0.447, MRR@10 from 0.586 to 0.595, and MAP@10 from 0.270 to 0.308 across all six datasets. The biggest improvement appears on Quati, where the mixed model clearly outperforms the legal-only one. Overall, the results show that legal-only and mixed training lead to different strengths: the first is better for specialization, while the second is more robust across different types of search, especially question-based ones. Both adapted models are available on Hugging Face
